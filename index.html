Welcome

Seal Team 6 is a collection of 3000 adorable, yet highly dangerous seals capable of completing contracts to earn Ethereum.

Each Baby seal is unique, and hand drawn with 74 different traits including weapons, clothing, colors and much more.

About seal Team 6

Seal Team 6 is a community-first project created to fund a community wallet, which in turn will use the talent of the web3 community to fund itself by providing the capital for high end crypto/NFT trades. Successful trades are used to pay holders and call makers. the amounts will be percentage based and are up to the community to allocate the best potential earnings.

The seal team 6 Community wallet will be an Ethereum based wallet funded by a percentage of mint profits.
